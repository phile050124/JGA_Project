Welcome to Our Community Gardening Initiative!

We believe in the power of food gardens to transform homes, schools, and neighborhoods. This website shares success stories, practical tips, and research focused on Gauteng’s food security efforts.

🌍 Our Mission

Our team of ICT students is working to support the United Nations' goals of No Poverty and Zero Hunger through accessible and sustainable food gardening practices.

🌿 Featured Garden Project: Siyakhana

Located in Johannesburg, Siyakhana is a flagship example of urban food resilience. With a blend of organic methods and community outreach, it feeds clinics and trains young farmers. See more projects →
