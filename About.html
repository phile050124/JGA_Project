Why We Started This Project

This website was created as part of the ICT First Year Group Assignment at the Faculty of Information and Communication Technology. Our goal is to explore how food gardening can be used as a tool for sustainable community development, aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals â€” especially SDG 1: No Poverty and SDG 2: Zero Hunger.

Project Objectives

  • Investigate food gardening practices across Gauteng
  • Analyze common challenges and solutions
  • Promote community participation and low-cost growing solutions
  • Develop a simple educational website using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Java
